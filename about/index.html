About

Hello! I'm Olivia. I'm a third-year PhD student in Computer Science working with Professor Ryan Kastner at UC San Diego. My research focuses on using hardware/software co-design to create efficient, fault-tolerant computer architectures for machine learning. My work is supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

Before coming to UCSD, I received my BS in Computer Science at the University of Chicago, where I worked with Professor Yanjing Li on mitigating cache side-channel attacks while maintaining cache performance and Professor Andrew A. Chien on integrating SSD latency predictors into cloud systems via hardware accelerators.
